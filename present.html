Hybrid System Redone

Still using the V6 Hybrid engines, Formula 1 has redesigned the power units to increase the amount of electrical power from 120kW to 350kW. This change is meant to improve efficiency and modern technology in the sport.

Venturi Tunnels Removed

The new rules have removed the venturi tunnels from the floor of the cars. Venturi tunnels were shaped to help create downforce by using airflow under the car. Removing them has changed how teams design their cars and create grip to the track.

New Teams Entering

Formula 1 is attracting new manufacturers and teams. 2 new teams, Cadillac and Audi. Their presense has increased the competition and raised the stakes.
